about the compnay
Established in August 2018 DROR is India’s first personal safety app with emergency services and healthcare facilities that helps citizens in distress by building community-based networking so that they can ensure each other’s safety. We promote women’s safety by leveraging technology and community insights to enable everyone in building a trust-able community. Our community-based organization is dedicated to creating a movement where we focus on women’s and children’s safety for a better and safer India. With DROR, every individual can get help as well as a rescue in just a click.

Over the past years, DROR has developed core features to suit the specific needs of the people, by addressing the real problems like Corona Virus pandemic. Through its breakthrough innovation like social distancing feature. DROR brings together all the safety features on a single platform as- your personal safety kit.

DROR safety app’s vision is to use technology to create a trust-based community network of vigilant and responsible citizens who are ready to help others in distress. It ensures the safety of the people, especially for women at all times by enabling instant SOS signals to mobilize help from around you, helping you identify safe spaces and meet new people and roam freely at any time without worrying
